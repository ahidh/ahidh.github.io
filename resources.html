ConforM Resources

ConforM works closely with many organizations and authorities to ensure proper EU and global regulations are adhered to. In order to provide accurate and expedited client guidance, ConforM is in constant communication with these groups to provide the highest level of professional service

Health Products Regulatory Authority (HPRA)

The Health Products Regulatory Authority (HPRA) is the Irish Competent Authority responsible for registration and monitoring the safety of medical devices in Ireland, among other European obligations.

In relation to ConforM, the role of the HPRA to designate and continuously monitor the performance of ConforM and other the Irish Notified Body.

The HPRA website provides valuable Regulatory Medical Devices information.
https://www.hpra.ie/homepage/medical-devices

European Commission for Medical Devices

The European Commission website provides key building blocks of information to allow for the regulatory frame work to function.
https://ec.europa.eu/growth/sectors/medical-devices_en

The following information can be found on its website:

  • Regulatory text
  • Guidance, such as MEDDEV
  • Market Surveillance
  • List of Harmonised Standards

International Medical Device Regulators Forum (IMDRF)

The International Medical Device Regulators (IMDRF) Forum publishes their documents and holds the archive of the GHTF documents.
http://www.imdrf.org/index.asp

International Accreditation Forum (IAF)

The International Accreditation Forum (IAF) publishes a mirage of document covering the central theme of audit practices. Documents IAF MD8 and IAF MD9 can be found in publications under the mandatory documents section.
http://www.imdrf.org/index.asp

TEAMNB

TEAMNB members, notified bodies, publish guidance documents starting with the prefix TEAM-NB and NB-MED.
http://www.team-nb.org/
